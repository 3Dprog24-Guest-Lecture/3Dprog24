\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Holds properties related to the visual appearance of a material. 

This struct encapsulates properties that define the visual characteristics of a material, including its base color and shininess, i.\+e (object final color \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} m\+Color).
